## Strategy strip

- **Problem:** teams repeat slow, manual document and data work that AI now does reliably — but only with governed tooling and someone to deploy it.
- **90-day proof point:** 2 teams live on the knowledge assistant with measured time savings by end of Q3.
- **One metric that matters:** verified hours saved / week, reported quarterly.
- **The ask:** champion access in 2 more business units next quarter; 30 min/month steering review.
