# Microsoft 365 Copilot — licensing

- Microsoft 365 Copilot Business: **$18.00 USD/user/month paid yearly** — promotional rate through 2026-09-30, standard rate $21.00; month-to-month $25.20. Requires an underlying Microsoft 365 Business plan. (as of 2026-07-12, source: https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-365-copilot/pricing)
- Enterprise-tier Copilot pricing and agent/consumption add-ons: not yet filed — verify against the same page (enterprise tab) before quoting in a business case.
