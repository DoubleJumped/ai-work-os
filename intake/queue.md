## Scoring notes

- Score honestly and write the number down the day the request arrives — a queue where everything is 75 is a queue that can't say no.
- Declines get a reply with the score and what would change it ("data isn't digitized yet — if that changes, resubmit"). The funnel builds trust only if it's transparent.
- U→E graduation happens at the monthly refresh, never ad hoc: it's a roadmap change and belongs in that conversation.
